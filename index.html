details

How to dress

Our wedding will be a fun and happy celebration! We encourage you to wear something quirky, vintage and colorful.

Please RSVP

We’d love for you to be there on this special day. However, seats will only be provided for those who RSVP. So please remember to mail yours by the deadline, June 20th!

Eat, drink, be merry

Enjoy a home cooked dinner, refresh yourself with drinks from our toonie bar, and indulge in a slice of heaven from our homemade cheesecake buffet! We just ask that you please do not drink and drive.

Unplugged ceremony

We all spend too much time in front of screens (*COUGH April…), so please turn off your devices during our ceremony. We have some lovely photographers and videographers to capture all the lovely moments.

Where to park

It can be a bit tricky to get up the hill to our venue, so make sure you drive up in a reliable vehicle. We ask that you please park on the gravel road. Request or offer a ride here.

Enjoy the outdoors

Live music, food, drinks, dance parties, a photobooth, and more! There will be plenty of activities for you to enjoy in our outdoor reception area. Access to the house is prohibited.
